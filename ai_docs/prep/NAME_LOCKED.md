**Why MicrobiomeGardener.ai?**
1. **Buyer Quality:** Attracts serious customers willing to pay $27-97/month (vs freebie seekers on gutfriendlygardening.com)
2. **Regulatory Freedom:** "Gardening platform" = no healthcare advertising restrictions
3. **Clear Positioning:** Immediately communicates the method (growing for microbiome)
4. **SEO Advantage:** Unique keyword combination with zero direct competition
5. **Identity-Based:** "Gardener" = who you become (stronger retention than object-based names)

**Why .ai TLD?**
1. Signals AI-powered technology
2. Expected for personalization platforms in 2026
3. Reinforces research-backed, scientific approach
4. Appeals to biohacker/longevity audience
